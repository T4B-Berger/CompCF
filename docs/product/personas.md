# CompCF Personas

## Organizer
- **Goals:** configure events, publish registrations, manage operations.
- **Permissions:** event configuration, publication controls, volunteer approvals, read audit summaries.
- **Frustrations:** hidden registration states, unclear volunteer staffing gaps.
- **Key workflows:** event setup, pricing/division review, publish, monitor execution.

## Staff
- **Goals:** execute day-of operations with minimal friction.
- **Permissions:** operational dashboards, schedule/heat tooling, scoring moderation.
- **Frustrations:** fragmented operational context and assignment ambiguity.
- **Key workflows:** heat readiness checks, assignment updates, issue triage.

## Judge
- **Goals:** accurate and fast score submission.
- **Permissions:** assigned-heat scoring and correction requests.
- **Frustrations:** unclear assignment, delayed correction handling.
- **Key workflows:** accept assignment, submit scores, file correction notes.

## Individual Athlete
- **Goals:** register quickly and track event status.
- **Permissions:** own profile/registration visibility.
- **Frustrations:** opaque lifecycle status and missing confirmations.
- **Key workflows:** registration creation, payment confirmation, schedule view.

## Team Captain
- **Goals:** coordinate team registration and readiness.
- **Permissions:** team registration management and roster updates.
- **Frustrations:** team-level status uncertainty.
- **Key workflows:** submit roster, manage registration states, view heats.

## Volunteer
- **Goals:** apply, onboard, and receive clear assignments.
- **Permissions:** volunteer profile, availability, assignment visibility.
- **Frustrations:** unclear acceptance/assignment communication.
- **Key workflows:** apply, update skills/availability, confirm shifts.

## Public User
- **Goals:** discover events and follow leaderboard outcomes.
- **Permissions:** public event and results views.
- **Frustrations:** stale schedules/scores.
- **Key workflows:** browse events, view heats/scores, follow leaderboard.

## Admin
- **Goals:** maintain policy, governance, and delivery integrity.
- **Permissions:** role matrix policy control, audit trail, quality gates.
- **Frustrations:** weak operational traceability.
- **Key workflows:** policy updates, compliance checks, release governance.
